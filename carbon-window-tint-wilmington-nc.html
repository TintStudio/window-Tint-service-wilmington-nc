Frequently Asked Questions

What is carbon window tint and how is it different from regular tint?
Carbon window tint uses carbon particles to block infrared light, offering superior heat rejection and UV protection compared to dyed tints. It doesn't fade over time, making it ideal for Wilmington NC’s sunny conditions.
Is carbon window tint legal in Wilmington, North Carolina?
Yes, carbon tints are legal as long as they meet North Carolina’s visible light transmission (VLT) laws. Front windows must allow over 70% light, while rear windows may be darker.
How long does carbon window tint typically last?
When installed professionally, carbon window tint can last 5 to 10 years without bubbling, fading, or peeling — even in Wilmington’s warm and humid climate.
Does carbon tint affect electronics or signals?
No, carbon tint is non-metallic, meaning it won’t interfere with GPS, radio, or cell phone signals — making it a great choice for modern vehicles in Wilmington NC.
What are the main benefits of installing carbon window tint?
Carbon tint provides excellent heat reduction, UV protection, glare control, and a sleek matte finish — all without the risk of fading or discoloration over time.
