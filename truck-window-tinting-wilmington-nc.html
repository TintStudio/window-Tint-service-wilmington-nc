Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of truck window tinting in Wilmington, NC?
Truck window tinting reduces heat and glare, protects the interior from sun damage, and enhances driver comfort on long Wilmington roads, making it a wise investment.
Are there specific tinting laws for trucks in Wilmington?
Yes, North Carolina requires front side windows to have at least 70% visible light transmission; rear windows have fewer restrictions, but it’s best to comply for safety and legality.
Can truck window tinting help improve fuel efficiency?
By reducing cabin heat, window tinting lessens the need for air conditioning, which can improve fuel economy—a practical benefit on Wilmington’s sunny highways.
How durable is truck window tint compared to car tint?
Truck window tint is equally durable when professionally installed, designed to withstand vibrations and the rugged conditions trucks often face.
How long does truck window tinting take?
Depending on the truck’s size and number of windows, tinting usually takes between two to three hours to ensure a flawless, long-lasting finish.
